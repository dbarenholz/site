    Hello, world!

    Hey there, and welcome to my little corner of the internet! My name is Daniël Barenholz, and I am a 25 year old PhD candidate at Utrecht University. While interested in many things, my research topic is the interplay between processes and data. If you want to learn more, I probably have some notes on the topic. For my academic publications, head to research. Looking for a more professional way of introducing myself? Go to the about section.

    Besides working, I can often be found behind my piano. I truly love music, from listening to it to trying to transcribe and to arrange. Once in a blue moon I may also code some tools, either for a community or for myself. I also have my own server with a bunch of fun tools running.
